Our Story

Masters of Some.
Extraordinary at All.

"Jack of all trades, master of none." Unlike this phrase our name is inspired by, Circus of Altrades aspires to push the boundaries of what the circus industry could look like for Singapore in the years to come.

We are Singapore's first homegrown group acrobatic circus troupe — a collective of multidisciplinary artists working across circus, dance, theatre, and physical performance. Founded and built locally, our core performers have trained, created, and grown together.

Beyond our own troupe, we maintain strong connections with international artistes, giving us the flexibility and reach to curate the perfect experience for any event — anywhere in the world.

Founder & Director
Koh Jia Sheng
Founder · Director · Multidisciplinary Artist

Koh Jia Sheng is a Singapore-based multidisciplinary artist working across circus, dance, and theatre. As the founder and director of Circus of Altrades, he creates original productions that blend contemporary performance with circus arts, developing distinctive works that bridge physical virtuosity and storytelling.

With a background in contemporary dance, acrobatics, and theatre, Jia Sheng has performed and collaborated across a wide range of productions, from national celebrations in Singapore to international stages in Indonesia, Malaysia, and Japan. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Theatre Studies, which informs his approach to directing and devising multidisciplinary performance.

Through Altrades, Jia Sheng continues to develop new circus work while mentoring emerging performers and expanding the possibilities of contemporary circus in Singapore.

Creative Director
Phiz Nasrudin
Creative Director · Fire Performer · Choreographer

Phiz Nasrudin has been in the performing arts industry for over 23 years, building a reputation as a versatile performer, choreographer, and creative visionary. As the Creative Director of Circus of Altrades, Singapore's first group acrobatic circus troupe, he leads the artistic direction and development of the troupe's unique performances.

Phiz is also Altrades' resident fire performer, known for delivering captivating and high-energy acts that blend danger, skill, and showmanship.

His portfolio as a choreographer includes MediaCorp's Let's Celebrate (2003 & 2004), and he has served as assistant choreographer for theatre productions such as Dim Sum Dollies (2024) and SOS Kumar (2025). Alongside performing, he is also an educator in hip hop dance for children, sharing his experience and passion with the next generation of performers.
