Who We Are

Singapore's Homegrown Circus Troupe

Circus of Altrades is Singapore's first homegrown group acrobatic circus troupe — a collective of multidisciplinary local artists working across circus, dance, theatre, and physical performance.

Beyond our own troupe, we maintain strong connections with world-class international artistes, giving us the flexibility and reach to curate the perfect experience for any event — anywhere in the world. Whatever your vision, whatever your stage, we make it happen.
