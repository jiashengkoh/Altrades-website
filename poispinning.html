♦   Stage Act

The Act

Precision, Grace & Spinning Fire

Our Poi Spinning stage act transforms any performance space into a theatre of light and motion. 1 to 4 skilled performers command the stage with poi — weighted balls on chains or cables — spinning them in hypnotic geometric patterns at breathtaking speed.

Available with fire poi for a dramatic outdoor spectacle, or LED poi for a dazzling indoor performance, the act is fully adaptable to your event's theme, mood, and setting. Each routine is choreographed to music, building from fluid, meditative motion to explosive, crowd-stopping finales.

♦
Fire Poi Blazing trails of fire that trace perfect arcs through the dark — primal, powerful, unmissable.
♦
LED Poi A dazzling light show built into every spin — perfect for indoor events and themed productions.
♦
Choreographed to Music Every routine is composed as a full performance arc with a choreographed build and finale.
♦
LED Pixel Poi Full-colour programmable LED poi that can display brand logos, patterns and colours — ideal for corporate events and brand activations.
♦
Fully Customisable Costume, music, and prop style tailored to match your event's look and feel.

♦   Roving Act

The Act

Moving Through the Crowd

Our Roving Poi act takes the magic off the stage and into your event floor. 1 to 6 performers weave through your guests, spinning poi in close quarters — creating spontaneous, intimate moments of wonder that a stage act simply can't replicate.

Whether spinning LED poi between cocktail tables or lighting up an outdoor walkway with fire poi, roving performers ensure every corner of your event feels alive. Guests don't just watch — they become part of the performance.

♦
Crowd Walkabout Performers weave between guests, bringing the act to your audience rather than the other way around.
♦
Fire & LED Options LED poi for indoor receptions; fire poi for outdoor evening events.
♦
Extended Duration 30–60 minute sets keep your event atmosphere buzzing from arrival to dinner call.
♦
Costume Customisable Styled to match your event's colour scheme, theme, or dress code.
